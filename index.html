About the Event

Mississippi at the center of AI's next chapter

Artificial Intelligence Workforce Development Economic Growth Higher Education

The Capital City Future Forum is a luncheon convening where Mississippi's students, educators, employers, and civic leaders come together to explore how AI is reshaping the classroom and the workplace. Hosted by Great City Mississippi Foundation and presented during the Mississippi Science Festival, the Forum brings national and Mississippi voices to the same table.

Why Jackson

The hub of an AI-ready state

Jackson sits at the center of the South and the center of Mississippi's university ecosystem. The capital and its metro are home to a concentration of talent few cities its size can claim.
